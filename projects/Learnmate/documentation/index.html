Time Organiser

You can track your events using the built-in calendar. For the first time ever - automatically organise your life. Just tell Learnmate when you're busy, and it will do the rest.

How to use

Adding an event

Click 'Add Event'-> Fill in start and end time

Removing an event

Select event to remove -> Click 'Remove Event'
Assignment Completion

Complete assignments with ease. No need to worry about lost homework. Hand in your assignments for marking in seconds.

How to use

Viewing your assignments

Homepage -> Click 'My assignments'

Submitting an assignment

Once practice lesson is complete, press 'Submit'.
Performance Tracking

Your motivating buddy. View your progress over time.

How to use

Viewing your progress graph

Homepage -> Click 'Performance Tracker'
Interactive Learning

Learn in seconds with the most effective methods driven by AI.

How to use

Adding a subject

Upon sign-up, select the subjects you study. Or, go into my account -> click 'my subjects'.

Beginning a session

From the homepage, click 'Learn and Practice' -> click the subject you wish to study -> click the topic(s) you wish to study -> click 'begin session'.
